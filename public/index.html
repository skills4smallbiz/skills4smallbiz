What Is CovidBizLink?

Recently, our communities (especially small businesses) have been grappling with the effects of COVID-19, and to help them out, we created CovidBizLink. It is a platform that helps connect small businesses with volunteers (accountants, developers, lawyers, etc) who are able to provide necessary services. Businesses need help with anything from web development to community outreach. If you want to help, no matter your background, sign up below. No matter how small your contribution may seem, it goes a long way!

Get Started

Making the Connection

After you sign up, be sure to fill out your information under "Account", either as a volunteer, a business, or even both! After you do so, head on over to the "Volunteer" page to see people that can help, or the "Business" page to see folks that need help. If you are signed in, the lists are filtered so that only the profiles relevant to you is displayed. Click on a listing that you are interested in, and a profile page will pop up with more details. Click on the "Contact" button to reach out and introduce yourself via email, or connect with them using their socials. This applies to both volunteers and businesses.
